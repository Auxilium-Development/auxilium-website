EzCC

Auxilium Team

Bringing ease to your control center, EzCC is centered around making your life easier. Whether it's system commands or an app you want to be able to launch from anywhere, EzCC provides it.

Description

EzCC brings custom modules into the brand new iOS 11 control center. EzCC is an all-in-one tweak that allows you to launch applications and perform system functions all from the control center!

Download

You can download EzCC by adding the repo:
repo.auxiliumdev.com
to Cydia, or you can press the button below on mobile to be redirected to Cydia directly!

ConfirmInstaLike

Tonyk7


If you lurk on Instagram, this tweak is perfect. Remove the risk of accidentally double-tapping a two year old post and liking it by downloading ConfirmInstaLike!

Description

Are you an Instagram lurker? If you are, you might get worried about accidentally double tapping a 2 year old post sometimes. ConfirmInstaLike puts a stop to liking an old post and emberassing yourself by requiring a confirmation if the post is older than 3 days.

Download

repo.auxiliumdev.com
